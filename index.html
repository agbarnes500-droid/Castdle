How to play

Guess the film from its cast

🎬
One film per day, the same for everyone. Cast members are revealed from a minor supporting role up to the lead.
🎯
You have 6 guesses. Each wrong guess reveals the next cast member.
💡
Hints are free — year, rating, genre, director, runtime, or tagline. They show in your shared result.
🔴
Hard mode hides character names — actor names only.
