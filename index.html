How to play

Guess the film from its cast

🎬
One film per day, the same for everyone. Cast members are revealed one at a time, from a minor supporting role up to the lead.
🎯
You have 6 guesses. Each wrong guess reveals the next cast member. Identify the film as early as you can.
💡
Hints are free — reveal the year, rating, genre, director or runtime whenever you're stuck. They'll show up in your shared result though.
🔴
Hard mode hides character names — you only see the actor, not the role they played.
